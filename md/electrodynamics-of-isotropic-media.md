# Maxwell's equations in vacuum

\begin{equation*}
\nabla \cdot \vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_{0}}
\end{equation*}

\begin{equation*}
\nabla \cdot \vec{B} = 0
\end{equation*}

\begin{equation*}
\nabla \times \vec{E} = -\frac{\partial B}{\partial t}
\end{equation*}

\begin{equation*}
\nabla \times \vec{B} = \frac{1}{c^{2}}\frac{\partial E}{\partial t} + \mu_{0}J
\end{equation*}
