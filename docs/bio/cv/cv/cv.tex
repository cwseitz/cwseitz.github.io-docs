\section{RESEARCH INTERESTS}  

My research is interdisciplinary and problem-oriented. I am currently developing novel methods for fast super-resolution microscopy to image the epigenetic landscape in living mammalian cell nuclei. I also have a healthy interest in deep probabilistic models, particularly diffusion models and their application in image restoration and \emph{de novo} drug design.
